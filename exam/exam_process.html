Overview

The examination process at Ideal College is designed to ensure fairness, integrity, and transparency in evaluating students' knowledge and skills.

Steps in the Examination Process

  1. Step 1: Registration

    Students must register for exams within the specified deadline.

  2. Step 2: Exam Dates

    Exam dates are communicated through exam notices on the Notices page.

  3. Step 3: Exam Venue

    Check the exam venue on the notice board and arrive at least 30 minutes before the exam.

  4. Step 4: Exam Conduct

    Exams are proctored to maintain academic integrity.

  5. Step 5: Grading

    Exams are graded promptly, and results are published on the college website.

  6. Step 6: Feedback

    Students receive feedback on their performance, which helps in continuous improvement.

Important Information

Students are required to adhere to the college's code of conduct during exams. Any violation of the code may result in disciplinary action.
