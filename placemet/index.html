Tips for Successful Placements

  • Build a Strong Resume: Craft a well-structured resume highlighting your skills, projects, and experiences.
  • Practice Interview Skills: Prepare for technical interviews and behavioral questions to excel in interviews.
  • Network Effectively: Attend career fairs, networking events, and connect with alumni in the industry.
  • Continuous Learning: Stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in the field of Computer Science.
  • Internships: Seek internships to gain practical experience and increase your employability.
